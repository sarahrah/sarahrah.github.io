Hello, nice to meet you! I'm Sarah - a Sydney-based FEWD / UX design student at General Assembly.

This year I'm making a big, all-in career change. I've been seduced by the world of User Experience as it appeals to my curious nature, obsession with clever design, and desire to create awesome things.

I could win awards for meticulousness and am inherently optimistic. I aspire to a minimalist lifestyle, but also can't stop buying new tech. For fun I practice yoga and knit sweaters. Zen af.

Ultimately I want to help solve user problems through simple, thoughtful and magical interactions.
